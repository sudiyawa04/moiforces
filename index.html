Who We Are

50 years of shaping Kenya's future, one student at a time

We're More Than Just a School

Since 1975, MFA has been the place where bright young Kenyans come to discover who they really are. Yes, we push our students academically - our results speak for themselves. But what really sets us apart is how we help each student find their own path, whether that's in the sciences, arts, sports, or leadership.

Walk through our campus on any given day and you'll see what makes us different. There's the Form 3 student helping a Form 1 with Math. The debate team practicing for nationals. Our cadet corps marching with pride. The art students painting the sunset. This is where hard work meets discovery, where discipline builds confidence, and where lifelong friendships are formed.
