Our Curriculum

Comprehensive Education for Holistic Development

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) & 8-4-4 System

Moi Forces Academy offers a robust academic program aligned with Kenya's national curriculum. We prepare students for excellence in both the CBC and 8-4-4 systems, ensuring they are well-equipped for higher education and beyond.

Our curriculum emphasizes:

  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Hands-on practical learning experiences
  • Technology integration across subjects
  • Character development and values education
  • Continuous assessment and feedback

Student Support Programs

Comprehensive Support for Holistic Student Development

Guidance and Counselling Program

At Moi Forces Academy, we understand that academic success goes hand-in-hand with emotional and mental well-being. Our comprehensive Guidance and Counselling Program provides students with the support, guidance, and resources they need to navigate their academic journey and personal development.

Weekly Motivational Sessions

Every Monday, students gather for inspiring talks from distinguished guest speakers, including professionals, alumni, motivational speakers, and industry leaders. These sessions cover topics such as career guidance, life skills, personal development, mental health awareness, and academic excellence strategies.

Individual Counselling

One-on-one sessions with trained counsellors to address personal challenges, academic concerns, career planning, and emotional well-being in a confidential and supportive environment.

Group Counselling & Workshops

Interactive group sessions and workshops on stress management, study skills, peer relationships, decision-making, and preparing for life after secondary school.

Career Guidance

Comprehensive career counselling, aptitude assessments, university selection guidance, and exposure to various career paths through career fairs and mentorship programs.
