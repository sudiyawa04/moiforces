Physics Laboratory

Our fully-equipped physics laboratory enables students to explore the fundamental laws of nature through practical experiments in mechanics, electricity, magnetism, optics, and modern physics.

Laboratory Equipment & Apparatus

Advanced oscilloscopes and multimeters
Mechanics apparatus (pulleys, inclined planes, springs)
Electricity & magnetism kits
Optics equipment (lenses, prisms, light sources)
Digital thermometers and calorimeters
Wave demonstration equipment
Data logging and analysis software
Safety equipment and first aid station

Key Experiments Conducted

  • Newton's Laws of Motion
  • Ohm's Law and Circuit Analysis
  • Refraction and Total Internal Reflection
  • Simple Harmonic Motion
  • Electromagnetic Induction
  • Heat Transfer and Thermodynamics

Chemistry Laboratory

A modern chemistry laboratory with fume hoods, safety showers, and comprehensive chemical storage. Students engage in practical experiments covering organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry.

Laboratory Equipment & Apparatus

Modern fume hoods with ventilation systems
Complete glassware sets (beakers, flasks, burettes)
Digital pH meters and electronic balances
Bunsen burners and heating equipment
Titration and volumetric analysis apparatus
Chemical reagent storage with safety protocols
Emergency shower and eyewash stations
Distillation and separation equipment

Key Experiments Conducted

  • Acid-Base Titrations
  • Qualitative Analysis (Salt Analysis)
  • Organic Chemistry Synthesis
  • Rates of Chemical Reactions
  • Redox Reactions and Electrochemistry
  • Preparation of Common Salts

Biology Laboratory

Our biology laboratory is equipped with advanced microscopes, dissection tools, and specimen collections. Students explore life sciences through observation, dissection, and experimentation.

Laboratory Equipment & Apparatus

40 compound microscopes (including digital)
Dissection kits and preserved specimens
Human anatomy models and charts
Microorganism culture equipment
Prepared slides collection (500+ slides)
Incubators and autoclaves
Plant and animal specimen collections
Digital imaging system for microscopy

Key Experiments Conducted

  • Cell Structure and Function Studies
  • Photosynthesis and Respiration Experiments
  • Dissection (Earthworm, Frog, Fish)
  • Osmosis and Diffusion Demonstrations
  • Enzyme Activity Analysis
  • Genetics and Inheritance Studies
  • Ecology and Environmental Studies

Safety Standards & Protocols

Student safety is our top priority

Protective Equipment

All students must wear lab coats, goggles, and appropriate protective gear during experiments

Emergency Equipment

Fire extinguishers, safety showers, eyewash stations, and first aid kits readily available

Trained Technicians

Qualified laboratory technicians supervise all practical sessions and maintain equipment

Safety Training

Comprehensive lab safety orientation for all students before practical work begins
