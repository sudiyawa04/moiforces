Our Curriculum

Comprehensive Education for Holistic Development

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) & 8-4-4 System

Moi Forces Academy offers a robust academic program aligned with Kenya's national curriculum. We prepare students for excellence in both the CBC and 8-4-4 systems, ensuring they are well-equipped for higher education and beyond.

Our curriculum emphasizes:

  • Critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Hands-on practical learning experiences
  • Technology integration across subjects
  • Character development and values education
  • Continuous assessment and feedback

We're Here to Support You

Because growing up is hard - you don't have to do it alone

Guidance and Counselling

Let's be honest - teenage years can be tough. Between exams, peer pressure, career decisions, and everything else life throws at you, it's normal to feel overwhelmed sometimes. That's exactly why Mrs. Mbaabu and our counselling team are here. This isn't about fixing you (you're not broken!). It's about having someone in your corner who listens, understands, and helps you figure things out.

Monday Motivation

Every Monday morning, we bring in someone interesting to talk to you - could be an MFA alumnus who's now a doctor, an entrepreneur sharing their startup journey, or a mental health expert giving practical tips. These aren't boring lectures. They're real conversations with real people who've been where you are.

One-on-One Talks

Sometimes you just need to talk to someone privately. Maybe you're struggling with a subject, dealing with family issues, or just feeling stressed. Whatever it is, you can book time with Mrs. Mbaabu or one of our counsellors. Everything stays confidential - this is your safe space.

Group Sessions & Workshops

Interactive group sessions and workshops on stress management, study skills, peer relationships, decision-making, and preparing for life after secondary school.

You know what? You're not the only one stressing about that exam or dealing with friendship drama. Group sessions help you realize others are going through similar things. We cover everything from study techniques that actually work, to managing stress without losing your mind, to figuring out life after Form 4.

Figuring Out Your Future

Medicine? Engineering? Teaching? Business? Or something completely different? We'll help you explore options, understand what different careers actually involve (not just what you see on TV), and make informed choices about university and your future. No pressure, just honest guidance.
