Application Process

Simple Steps to Join MFA

1

Obtain Application Form

Download the application form from our website or collect it from the school office. Application fee: KES 2,000.

Download Form
2

Complete Application

Fill in the application form accurately and attach all required documents including:

  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Academic transcripts/report forms
  • Recent passport photos (2)
  • Medical examination report
3

Submit Application

Submit your completed form and documents to the admissions office or send via email to admissions@mfa.ac.ke

4

Entrance Assessment

Attend the entrance examination and interview. You'll receive notification of the date and time via SMS and email.

5

Admission Letter

Successful candidates receive an admission letter within 2 weeks of the assessment. Accept the offer within 14 days.

6

Complete Registration

Pay the required fees and complete registration. Attend orientation for new students and parents.

Admission Requirements

Entry Criteria for Different Levels

Form One (Grade 9)

Academic Requirements:

  • Minimum KCPE score: 300 marks
  • Strong performance in core subjects
  • Primary school leaving certificate

Additional Requirements:

  • Age: 13-15 years
  • Good conduct record
  • Medical fitness certificate

Transfer Students

Academic Requirements:

  • Good academic standing (Mean grade C+)
  • Recent academic transcripts
  • Transfer letter from previous school

Additional Requirements:

  • Disciplinary clearance
  • Entrance assessment test
  • Interview with principal

International Students

Academic Requirements:

  • Equivalent academic credentials
  • English proficiency proof
  • Credential evaluation report

Additional Requirements:

  • Valid passport and student visa
  • Guardian in Kenya
  • Health insurance coverage

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Supporting Talented Students

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Up to 50% Tuition

Awarded to students with exceptional KCPE scores (380+) and outstanding academic performance.

  • KCPE score of 380 and above
  • Maintained through academic excellence
  • Renewable annually

Sports & Talent Scholarship

Up to 30% Tuition

For students with exceptional talent in sports, music, drama, or other co-curricular activities.

  • National/regional recognition
  • Continued participation required
  • Performance-based renewal

Need-Based Bursary

Variable Support

Financial assistance for deserving students from families with demonstrated financial need.

  • Based on financial assessment
  • Supporting documents required
  • Reviewed each academic year
