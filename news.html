Academic Excellence January 15, 2025

MFA Achieves Outstanding KCSE Results 2024

Moi Forces Academy has once again demonstrated academic excellence with an impressive 98% success rate in the 2024 KCSE examinations. Our students achieved a mean score of 8.5, with 85% qualifying for university admission.

The school produced 15 students with straight As, and excelled particularly in Mathematics, Sciences, and Languages. Principal Col. (Rtd) David Kimani attributed the success to dedicated teachers, supportive parents, and hardworking students.
