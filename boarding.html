Welcome to Our Boarding Community

Where Students Thrive in a Caring, Structured Environment

At Moi Forces Academy, our boarding program offers more than just accommodation—it provides a nurturing environment where students develop independence, build lifelong friendships, and grow into responsible citizens. Our six houses create a family atmosphere within the larger school community, each with its own unique identity and proud traditions.

Leadership

Meet Our Boarding Master

Mr. Patrick Mwangi

Head of Boarding

"As Boarding Master, my primary goal is to ensure that every student feels safe, supported, and valued. Our boarding community is built on mutual respect, discipline, and care. We work tirelessly to create an environment where students can excel academically while developing the life skills necessary for success beyond school."

Mr. Patrick Mwangi
Boarding Master, Moi Forces Academy

Spiritual & Religious Life

Nurturing Faith and Values

At MFA, we recognize the importance of spiritual development alongside academic and physical growth. We provide a supportive environment for students to practice their faith and develop strong moral values.

Christian Union (CU)

Growing in Faith Together

The MFA Christian Union is a vibrant fellowship that brings together students from various Christian denominations. We focus on spiritual growth, worship, and service to the community.

Activities & Programs

  • Sunday Services: Weekly worship and fellowship - 9:00 AM
  • Bible Study: Wednesday evenings - 5:30 PM
  • Choir Practice: Tuesday & Thursday - 4:00 PM
  • Prayer Meetings: Daily morning devotions - 6:30 AM
  • Outreach Programs: Monthly community service projects
  • Youth Conferences: Quarterly spiritual enrichment events

Leadership

Patron: Rev. John Kariuki
Chairperson: Faith Wanjiru (Form 4)
Active Members: 450+ students

Muslim Students Association (MUMSA)

United in Faith and Brotherhood

MUMSA provides a welcoming space for Muslim students to practice their faith, learn about Islam, and support one another. We foster understanding, tolerance, and respect across the entire school community.

Activities & Programs

  • Daily Prayers (Salah): Prayer room available throughout the day
  • Jumu'ah (Friday Prayer): Congregational prayer - 1:00 PM
  • Islamic Studies: Thursday sessions - 5:00 PM
  • Quran Recitation: Weekend classes and competitions
  • Ramadan Programs: Special iftar meals and Taraweeh prayers
  • Eid Celebrations: Community festivities for Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha
  • Charity Drives: Regular zakat and sadaqah initiatives

Leadership

Patron: Sheikh Ahmed Hassan
Chairperson: Amina Mohamed (Form 4)
Active Members: 180+ students

Interfaith Harmony

At MFA, we celebrate our diversity and promote mutual respect and understanding between different faith communities. Our students regularly participate in joint community service projects and interfaith dialogue sessions, fostering unity and respect for all beliefs.
