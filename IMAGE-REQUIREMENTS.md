# Image Requirements & Guidelines for MFA Website

## 📸 Required Images Checklist

This document outlines all images needed for the Moi Forces Academy website, including specifications and recommendations for sourcing or creating them.

---

## 🎯 Priority Images (Must Have)

### 1. **School Logo** ✅ 
- **File**: `images/logo.png`
- **Status**: Already present
- **Usage**: Header, footer, favicon

### 2. **Hero Slider Images** (3 images)
- **Files**: `hero-1.jpg`, `hero-2.jpg`, `hero-3.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 1080px (16:9 ratio)
- **Format**: JPEG, optimized
- **File Size**: < 500KB each
- **Content Suggestions**:
  - hero-1.jpg: Main school building/campus aerial view
  - hero-2.jpg: Students in classroom/laboratory
  - hero-3.jpg: Students in sports/co-curricular activities

### 3. **About Preview Image**
- **File**: `about-preview.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 800 x 600px
- **Content**: Students in school uniform, preferably group photo

### 4. **Co-curricular Activity Cards** (4 images)
- **Files**: `sports.jpg`, `cadets.jpg`, `music.jpg`, `clubs.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 800 x 600px each
- **Content**:
  - sports.jpg: Students playing football, basketball, or athletics
  - cadets.jpg: Students in cadet uniform during drill or training
  - music.jpg: School band, choir, or drama performance
  - clubs.jpg: Students in club activities (science club, debate, etc.)

### 5. **News & Events Thumbnails** (3 images)
- **Files**: `news-1.jpg`, `news-2.jpg`, `news-3.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 600 x 400px
- **Content**:
  - news-1.jpg: Academic ceremony/awards
  - news-2.jpg: Sports event/championship
  - news-3.jpg: School event/open day

### 6. **Testimonial Photos** (3 images)
- **Files**: `alumni-1.jpg`, `alumni-2.jpg`, `parent-1.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 150 x 150px (square)
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG
- **Content**: Professional headshots (can use placeholder or stock photos initially)

### 7. **Call-to-Action Background**
- **File**: `cta-bg.jpg`
- **Dimensions**: 1920 x 600px
- **Content**: School campus, parade ground, or inspiring school scene
- **Note**: Will have dark overlay applied

## 📋 Image Specifications

### Technical Requirements

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Format** | JPEG for photos, PNG for logos/graphics |
| **Color Space** | sRGB |
| **Resolution** | 72 DPI (web standard) |
| **Compression** | 80-85% quality for JPEG |
| **File Naming** | Lowercase, hyphens (e.g., `hero-image.jpg`) |
| **Alt Text** | Required for accessibility |

## 📸 Photography Guidelines

### Do's ✅
- Use high-resolution original photos
- Capture students in natural learning environments
- Show diversity and inclusion
- Ensure good lighting and composition
- Get proper permissions/model releases
- Include school branding where appropriate
- Show pride, discipline, and excellence

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use heavily filtered or manipulated images
- Avoid poor lighting or blurry photos
- Don't include identifiable students without permission
- Avoid stock photos that look too generic
- Don't use images from other schools
- Avoid cluttered or distracting backgrounds

## 🔍 Image Sourcing Options

### Option 1: School Photography
**Best Choice** - Use actual photos from MFA:
- School events
- Classroom activities
- Sports competitions
- Cadet parades
- Campus buildings
- Student life

**How to Obtain**:
1. Contact school photographer
2. Review school archives
3. Organize professional photo shoot
4. Student/parent submitted photos (with permission)

### Option 2: Professional Photography
Hire a professional photographer for:
- Campus shots
- Team photos
- Activity documentation
- Event coverage

**Estimated Cost**: KES 15,000 - 50,000 for full shoot

### Option 3: Stock Photos (Temporary Solution)
Use as placeholders while gathering real photos:

**Free Stock Photo Sites**:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) - Search: "school", "students", "education"
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com/) - High quality, free
- [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/) - Large variety

**Paid Stock Sites** (Higher quality):
- [Shutterstock](https://shutterstock.com/)
- [iStock](https://istockphoto.com/)
- [Adobe Stock](https://stock.adobe.com/)

**Search Keywords**:
- "Kenyan school students"
- "Secondary school classroom"
- "School sports activities"
- "Students in uniform"
- "School campus aerial"

### Option 4: AI-Generated Images (Modern Approach)
Tools like:
- Midjourney
- DALL-E
- Stable Diffusion

**Note**: Ensure generated images look realistic and appropriate

## 🚀 Quick Start: Getting Images Ready

### Step 1: Gather Photos
Collect all available MFA photos from:
- School archives
- Recent events
- Social media (with permission)
- Professional photographer

### Step 2: Organize
Sort photos into categories:
- Campus/Buildings
- Students/Activities
- Staff/Leadership
- Events

### Step 3: Edit & Resize
Using your preferred tool:
1. Crop to correct aspect ratio
2. Resize to recommended dimensions
3. Adjust brightness/contrast if needed
4. Save with quality 80-85%

### Step 4: Optimize
Run through compression tool:
- Target: 50-70% size reduction
- Maintain visual quality

### Step 5: Rename & Upload
- Use descriptive names
- Upload to `images/` folder
- Test on website
