About Moi Forces Academy

A Legacy of Excellence, Discipline, and Leadership

Building Future Leaders

Moi Forces Academy stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Kenya, combining rigorous academic standards with military discipline and character development. Founded with the vision of producing well-rounded citizens, our institution has consistently delivered outstanding results while nurturing leadership, patriotism, and moral values.

We pride ourselves on our holistic approach to education, where academic achievement goes hand-in-hand with physical fitness, artistic expression, and community service. Our students emerge as confident, disciplined, and compassionate leaders ready to serve their nation and the world.
