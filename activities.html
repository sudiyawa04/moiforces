Holistic Development

At MFA, we believe in nurturing talents beyond academics

Our comprehensive co-curricular program provides students with opportunities to discover and develop their talents, build leadership skills, foster teamwork, and maintain physical fitness. From competitive sports to creative arts, cadet training to community service, every student finds their passion.

Cadet Training Program

Building Discipline, Leadership & Patriotism

Our cadet program is a cornerstone of character development at MFA, instilling military discipline, leadership qualities, and a deep sense of patriotism in every student.

Weekly Drills

Regular marching drills and physical training sessions to build discipline and teamwork.

Rank Progression

Merit-based rank advancement from recruit to cadet leaders with increased responsibilities.

Field Camps

Annual camps with survival skills, navigation, first aid, and leadership exercises.

Ceremonial Duties

Participation in school and national events, parades, and flag-raising ceremonies.

Recent Achievements:

  • Best Cadet Unit - National Competition 2024
  • Excellence Award in Drill & Discipline
  • 3 Students selected for National Cadet Leadership
