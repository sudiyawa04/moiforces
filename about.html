Our Rich History

Five Decades of Educational Excellence

1975

The Beginning

Moi Forces Academy was established to provide quality education for children of service personnel and civilians, emphasizing discipline and academic excellence.

1980s

Growth & Expansion

The academy expanded its facilities, adding modern classrooms, laboratories, and sports facilities to accommodate growing student numbers.

1990s

Academic Recognition

MFA achieved national recognition for outstanding KCSE results, establishing itself among Kenya's top-performing schools.

2000s

Modernization

Introduction of ICT programs, upgraded science laboratories, and implementation of modern teaching methodologies.

2020s

Future Ready

Continued excellence with state-of-the-art facilities, competency-based curriculum, and holistic student development programs.

Our Mission

To provide world-class education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and leadership skills, preparing students to become responsible citizens and leaders in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution of academic excellence in Kenya and the region, recognized for producing disciplined, patriotic, and innovative leaders who positively transform society.

Our Core Values

The Pillars of Our Institution

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all endeavors, encouraging students to achieve their full potential academically, physically, and morally.

Discipline

Military-style discipline forms the foundation of our character development, instilling self-control, respect, and responsibility in every student.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all aspects of school life, building trust and moral character.

Patriotism

We nurture love for country and national pride, preparing students to serve Kenya with dedication and loyalty.

Teamwork

We foster collaboration, unity, and mutual respect through co-curricular activities and community service.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, problem-solving, and adaptability to prepare students for future challenges.

Principal's Message

Col. (Rtd) John Kamau

School Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Visitors,

It is with great pride and honor that I welcome you to Moi Forces Academy, an institution that has stood as a beacon of academic excellence, discipline, and patriotism for over five decades.

At MFA, we believe that education extends far beyond the classroom. Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals who excel not only academically but also in character, leadership, and service to society. We are committed to nurturing each student's unique talents and potential while instilling the values of integrity, discipline, and patriotism.

Our dedicated team of educators works tirelessly to create an environment where learning is both challenging and enjoyable. We combine rigorous academic programs with extensive co-curricular activities to ensure holistic development. From our state-of-the-art laboratories to our sports fields and cultural programs, every aspect of MFA is designed to inspire excellence.

The foundation of our success lies in our core values and the strong partnership between the school, students, and parents. Together, we create a supportive community where every child can thrive and discover their path to greatness.

I invite you to explore our website and discover what makes Moi Forces Academy truly exceptional. Whether you're a prospective student, parent, or simply interested in learning more about our institution, I am confident you will find that MFA offers an unparalleled educational experience.

"Excellence Through Discipline" is not just our motto—it's our way of life.

Col. (Rtd) John Kamau
Principal, Moi Forces Academy
